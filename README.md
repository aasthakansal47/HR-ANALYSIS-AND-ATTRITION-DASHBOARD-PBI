# 📊 HR Analytics & Attrition Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 🔍 Overview
This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. The dashboard helps analyze employee attrition patterns and key HR metrics to support data-driven decision-making.

It provides insights into workforce trends such as attrition by gender, age, salary, education, and years at the company.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify key factors affecting employee turnover
- Provide actionable insights for HR decision-making
- Visualize workforce distribution and demographics

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset contains employee-level information including:
- Employee ID
- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Salary
- Years at Company
- Education Field
- Attrition Status

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key KPIs
- Total Employees: 1470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Average Age: 37
- Average Salary: ₹6.5K
- Average Years at Company: 7

---

### 🔹 Visualizations
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Education Field
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Department Filter (HR, R&D, Sales)

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Excel / CSV Dataset
- Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Highest attrition observed in early years (1–2 years)
- Employees with lower salaries have higher attrition
- Age group 26–35 shows maximum attrition
- Life Sciences & Medical fields dominate attrition

---

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Download the `.pbix` file
2. Open in Power BI Desktop
3. Interact with filters and visuals
